FAQ
Common questions
What is black letter law and why does it matter for the bar exam? +
Black letter law refers to the core, well-established legal principles and rules that are universally tested on the bar exam. Mastering these rules, not just recognizing them, is one of the highest-leverage things you can do to improve your MBE and MEE scores.
How is Lexora Prep different from Barbri or Themis? +
Barbri and Themis are full-service courses. Lexora Prep is a specialized memorization engine focused on black letter law retention, with spaced repetition, daily smart plans, and subject analytics. It works as a standalone product or as a supplement to any course you are already using.
Can I use Lexora Prep alongside my existing bar prep course? +
Absolutely. Many students use Lexora Prep as a daily memorization layer on top of their main course. The smart plan integrates with your exam date and adjusts automatically around your schedule.
Do you offer refunds? +
Yes. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If it is not the right fit, contact support within 7 days of purchase.
Which bar exam does Lexora Prep cover? +
Lexora Prep currently covers all 12 UBE subjects including the 7 MBE subjects. State-specific content for select jurisdictions is on the roadmap for late 2026.
